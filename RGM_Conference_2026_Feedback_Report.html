Key takeaways

Biggest strength

All three breakout sessions scored 4.83/5. Peer networking, the awards dinner, and the karaoke afterparty were the most praised moments.

Top opportunity

Food & beverage (3.92) and venue (4.45) are the lowest-rated categories. A dance floor and extended event duration were the most common requests.

Content demand

P&L and Financials remain the #1 requested future topic. Power BI, career advancement, and hiring/scheduling are also in strong demand.

Plus-one & afterparty

7 of 12 said bringing a plus-one enhanced their experience. Of afterparty attendees, all reported connecting with peers from other stores.
